Into the Sacred Peaks

Far beyond the veil of ordinary sight, where ancient stone pierces the celestial realm, the masters have gathered since time immemorial. Their breath mingles with cloud and starlight, their stillness more profound than the deepest mountain root.

Here, in the silence between heartbeats, the universe speaks its oldest truths.

The Great Silence

Beyond the Mind

The clouds drift. The mountain stands. Between the drifting and the standing — that is where the yogi rests. Not in thought, not in memory, not in the future's dream, but in the luminous present that has no beginning and no end.

"The Self is the witness, not the witnessed."
